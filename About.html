About Us

Our Objectives and Mission

  • Recieve Donations and funds to feed needy people.
  • Supply and distribute food grains and vegetables to needy and poor people.
  • Help vulnerable people to get food they need to survive and therein.
  • To establish, promote and carry-out SOCIAL ECONOMIC CULTURAL EDUCATION activities to improve living conditions of Rural/urban poor, downtrodden with special attention to women and children and community at large.
  • To establish, promote and carry-out Army & Police training programmes and coaching centers for rural unemployed youths.
  • To establish, promote and carry-out activities of teaching, training, research and extension education to spread the knowledge and technology from lab to land in the field of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, horticulture, health, sanitation, rural artisan and folk, ancient, science, ayurveda, yoga, holistic, devine and spiritual arena.
  • To establish, promote and carry-out to empower the Rural/Urban poor and community at large with Special attention to promote and nurture Women Self Help Groups (SHG's) Economically weaker sections, Educated unemployed youths.
  • To establish, promote and carry-out the Health programmes etc, including Mass Education Seminars to fight against HIV/Aids, Polio and Leprosy and such other deadly diseases and to promote Healthy living sanitation, safe and clean drinking water, Family planning, Nature cure, Yoga, holistic, devine and spiritual treatments.
  • To fight against social evils, abuse and exploitation of children, physically and mentally retarted and challenged men and women, child labor, antisocial activities and facilities and Promote National Integration Social Justice and Universal Brotherhood.
  • To promote and inculate the habit of saving, thirft and resources mobilization among community with special attention to women and children and to facilitate economic empowerment through self-help concept.
  • To take up any social and welfare activities as trust determines from time to time.
